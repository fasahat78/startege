### Level 1-10: Beginner (Foundation)

## Concept Complexity Examples

### Simple (Levels 1-10)
- Single concept
- Clear definition
- No prerequisites
- Direct questions

### Moderate (Levels 11-20)
- 2-3 related concepts
- Application required
- Some prerequisites
- Scenario questions

### Complex (Levels 21-30)
- 3-4 integrated concepts
- Analysis required
- Multiple prerequisites
- Complex scenarios

### Expert (Levels 31-40)
- 4+ concepts synthesis
- Framework design
- Extensive prerequisites
- Real-world problems
