## üìä Feedback Collection Framework

### In-App Feedback System

#### Quick Feedback Widget
**Location**: Every page (bottom right corner)  
**Types**:
- üêõ Bug Report
- üí° Feature Request
- ‚ùì Question
- üòä General Feedback

**Implementation**:
- One-click feedback button
- Categorize automatically
- Track per user

#### Post-Action Surveys
**Triggers**:
- After completing an exam
- After using Startegizer
- After onboarding
- After 7 days of usage

**Questions** (2-3 max):
- "How helpful was [feature]?" (1-5 stars)
- "What would make this better?" (open text)
- "Would you recommend Startege?" (Yes/No)

### Structured Feedback Collection

#### Week 1 Survey (5 minutes)
**Sent**: After 7 days of usage  
**Questions**:
1. How clear was the onboarding? (1-5)
2. What's your favorite feature?
3. What's confusing or missing?
4. Would you pay $19/month? (Yes/No/Maybe)
5. What's the maximum you'd pay?

**Incentive**: 100 bonus AI credits for completion

#### Month 1 Survey (10 minutes)
**Sent**: After 30 days  
**Questions**:
1. NPS Score (0-10)
2. Which features do you use most?
3. What features are missing?
4. How likely to recommend? (1-10)
5. What would make you cancel?
6. What would make you upgrade?

**Incentive**: 200 bonus AI credits + chance to win annual subscription

#### Exit Survey (If Canceling)
**Questions**:
1. Why are you canceling?
2. What would make you stay?
3. Would you come back if [feature] was added?
4. What did you like most?

**Incentive**: 50% off for 3 months if they stay

### User Interviews

#### Target: 20-30 interviews in first 90 days

**Recruitment**:
- Email to active users
- Offer: $50 Amazon gift card or 3 months free
- Schedule via Calendly

**Structure** (30 minutes):
1. **Introduction** (5 min)
   - Thank them for participating
   - Explain purpose

2. **Usage Questions** (10 min)
   - How are you using Startege?
   - What features do you use most?
   - What's your learning goal?

3. **Feedback** (10 min)
   - What do you love?
   - What's frustrating?
   - What's missing?

4. **Pricing** (5 min)
   - Is the price fair?
   - What would you pay?
   - What would make it worth more?

**Compensation**:
- $50 Amazon gift card OR
- 3 months free premium

### Feedback Dashboard

**For You**:
- Real-time feedback feed
- Categorization (Bug, Feature, UX, Content)
- User sentiment analysis
- Top requests tracking

**For Users**:
- See their feedback status
- Track feature requests they submitted
- See when their suggestions are implemented
